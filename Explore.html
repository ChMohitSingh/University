Explore Jaatshakti University

Academic Programs

Jaatshakti University offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. Our academic departments include:

  • University of Engineering and Technology
  • University of Business and Management
  • University of Law Management
  • University of Arts and Humanities
  • University of Health Sciences
  • University of Politics

Research and Innovation

At Jaatshakti University, we foster a culture of research and innovation. Our faculty and students actively engage in cutting-edge research projects, contributing to advancements in various fields. We provide state-of-the-art research facilities and resources to support their endeavors.

Campus Facilities

Our sprawling campus is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities to create an optimal learning environment. Some of the key facilities include:

  • Well-equipped laboratories for practical learning
  • Libraries stocked with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources
  • Auditoriums and seminar halls for academic and cultural events
  • Sports complex and gymnasium to promote physical fitness
  • Cafeterias and food courts offering a variety of cuisines
  • Hostel accommodation for students, with separate facilities for male and female students
  • Wi-Fi connectivity across the campus

Extracurricular Activities

Jaatshakti University recognizes the importance of holistic development and offers a wide range of extracurricular activities. Students can participate in various clubs, societies, and student organizations related to their interests, such as:

  • Music and Dance Club
  • Sports Club
  • Debate and Public Speaking Society
  • Entrepreneurship Club
  • Cultural and Artistic Societies

Career Services

We have a dedicated Career Services department that assists students in their career planning and job placement. The department organizes career fairs, workshops, and counseling sessions to help students explore career options, develop job-search skills, and connect with potential employers.

Global Opportunities

Jaatshakti University promotes global exposure and encourages students to engage in international programs. We have partnerships with renowned universities and institutions worldwide, offering opportunities for student exchange programs, study abroad programs, and research collaborations.

Community Engagement

As a socially responsible institution, Jaatshakti University actively engages with the local community through various outreach programs and initiatives. We encourage our students to participate in community service activities and contribute to the betterment of society.
